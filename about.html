About Me

I didn’t always know I wanted to be a designer. I started out exploring graphic design, photography, and videography—anything that let me create. But when my sister introduced me to UI/UX, something clicked. At first, I was drawn to the visuals, but I quickly fell in love with the problem-solving side of it—the challenge of making something both beautiful and meaningful.

My process is intuitive and iterative; every choice I make has a reason behind it. I want my work to feel thoughtful, clear, and usable—the kind of design that makes sense to everyone. I’m constantly learning, growing, and pushing myself to get better. One day, I hope to work in a space that’s professional, forward-thinking, and driven by innovation.
